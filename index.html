About Us

With over two decades of expertise in kosher wine and spirits, Wine on the Hills proudly serves the heart of Woodridge, NY. Our curated collection is the result of years of experience, industry relationships, and a deep appreciation for fine kosher wines and liquors.

We specialize in sourcing unique and high-quality kosher selections from around the world — from classic Israeli vineyards to hidden gems in France, Italy, and California.

Whether you’re preparing for Shabbos, a simcha, or simply exploring new flavors, our knowledgeable staff is here to help you find the perfect bottle. All products are certified kosher, with Mevushal and non-Mevushal options clearly labeled.
