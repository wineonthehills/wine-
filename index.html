About Us

Wine on the Hills is more than just a wine store — it's a new destination for the kosher community in the Catskills to explore, celebrate, and enjoy premium wines and spirits with confidence and joy.

Located in the Center One Shopping Center in Woodridge, New York, our brand-new, beautifully designed store offers an inviting, boutique-style experience with a focus on quality, service, and tradition. From the moment you walk through our doors, you’ll feel the warmth and refinement that makes Wine on the Hills truly special.

We specialize exclusively in kosher-certified wines and spirits — hand-selected from Israel, France, California, and around the world. Whether you're planning a simcha, enhancing your Shabbos table, or selecting a meaningful gift, we are here to guide you through a thoughtfully curated selection that balances tradition with contemporary taste.

At Wine on the Hills, our mission is to provide not only the finest kosher wines and liquors but also an elevated shopping experience. Our knowledgeable staff is passionate about helping every customer — whether you're a seasoned wine enthusiast or just getting started — discover new favorites and make confident choices.

We are proud to support the community with reliable service, fair pricing, and a sincere commitment to quality. We invite you to stop by, browse our collection, and experience the newest gem in the Catskills kosher scene. You’ll find everything from world-renowned labels to boutique kosher producers — always beautifully presented and backed by friendly, personal attention.

Whether you're visiting for the summer, hosting a celebration, or stocking up for Yom Tov, Wine on the Hills is here to make every occasion more tasteful and memorable.
