Wine on the Hills is a boutique kosher wine and spirits store located in the heart of the Catskills at the Center One Kosher Shopping Center, Woodridge, NY.

We are proud to offer a curated selection of top-quality kosher wines and spirits from Israel, California, France, and beyond — perfect for your Shabbat table, simchas, or quiet evenings at home.

With a strong focus on customer service and a deep understanding of the kosher wine market, our friendly team is here to help you find the perfect bottle for any occasion. Whether you're a connoisseur or just exploring, we’re here to guide you.

We welcome you to stop by and experience a warm atmosphere, helpful service, and premium kosher selection — all under one roof.
